# Play Pack Pilot product definition

## Product

Play Pack Pilot is an AI-powered sweepstakes and giveaway discovery SaaS at
`https://playpackpilot.com`.

It scans explicitly approved public sources, records source evidence, extracts
and normalizes promotion information, summarizes official rules, estimates
entry effort and listing risk, evaluates a user's likely eligibility, ranks
opportunities, and helps users save, track, and receive reminders for them. The
user visits the original sponsor site to enter.

## Non-negotiable boundary

Play Pack Pilot does not:

- sponsor or administer promotions;
- accept or automatically submit entries;
- select winners, fund prizes, hold prizes, or distribute prizes;
- collect entry fees or sell improved odds;
- represent AI analysis as legal advice, certification, guaranteed eligibility,
  or a guarantee of winning;
- bypass source access controls, CAPTCHAs, authentication, anti-bot systems,
  robots/terms review, or rate limits.

Every opportunity surface must retain the original sponsor, official promotion
URL, official-rules URL when available, source attribution when required, last
verification time, and the statement that Play Pack Pilot is not the sponsor.

## Primary users

- Individuals who want a trustworthy discovery and tracking workflow.
- Power users who want personalized ranking, rules analysis, reminders, and
  approved custom scans.
- Households using the Squadron plan with separate profiles and a shared
  workspace.
- Platform operators who review sources, low-confidence extraction, duplicate
  candidates, risk flags, billing events, and system health.

## Pilot Credits

Pilot Credits are internal usage units. They have no cash value, are not
transferable or redeemable, do not buy entries or prizes, and cannot affect odds.
Normal browsing, filtering, saving, and opening sponsor links do not consume
credits. A server-side append-only ledger is authoritative for grants,
consumption, expiration, correction, and idempotent refunds.

## Product language

Public headline: **Discover More. Miss Less. Enter Smarter.**

Primary public CTA: **Start Scanning**

Primary outbound opportunity CTA: **Visit Official Sweepstakes**

Application navigation may use aviation language when clarity is retained:
Flight Deck (dashboard), Radar (discovery), Hangar (saved), Mission Log
(user-reported entry status), Flight Plan (calendar/reminders), Co-Pilot (AI),
Alerts, Pilot Credits, Billing, and Settings.
